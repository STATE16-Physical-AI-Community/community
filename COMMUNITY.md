# Community

The STATE16 Physical AI Community is an open professional community for people interested in the reliability, safety, and physical validity of AI systems that interact with the real world.

The community welcomes:

- Researchers
- Engineers
- Developers
- Students
- Robotics practitioners
- Drone experts
- AI safety researchers
- Domain experts

The community is focused on discussion, learning, research exploration, open benchmarks, technical examples, and shared terminology around Physical AI Assurance.

Members can contribute by:

- Reviewing papers
- Suggesting failure modes
- Proposing benchmarks
- Writing documentation
- Creating toy examples
- Participating in discussions
- Presenting short demos
- Joining community meetings

GitHub Discussions are the main place for community conversations.
