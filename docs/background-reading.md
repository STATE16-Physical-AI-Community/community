# Background Reading

This page collects selected background materials relevant to the STATE16 Physical AI Community.

The community focuses on open discussion, learning, paper reviews, technical vocabulary, and broad research exploration around Physical AI Assurance.

## Recommended starting point

### Silent Failures in Physical AI: A Literature Review of Runtime Action Authorization for Autonomous Systems

This literature review discusses the need for runtime action authorization in Physical AI systems. It focuses on the gap between black-box AI model outputs and physical execution, and surveys related work across runtime assurance, safety filters, embodied AI, VLA models, world models, guardrails, uncertainty, and autonomous systems.

Key themes:

- Silent failures in Physical AI
- Runtime action authorization
- State validity
- Physical feasibility
- Operational constraints
- Fallback behavior
- Auditability
- Evaluation of guardrail mechanisms

This paper can serve as broad background for community discussions.

Link: [[arXiv]](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.00090)
